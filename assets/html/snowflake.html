Draw your own fold-n-cut design


Get a piece of square paper. Turn it rightside up (i.e. you should be facing the "pretty" side!)


Fold the paper in half diagonally to make a triangle.


Fold the paper in half diagonally again.


Divide the triangle into three parts (you can use a protractor for extra precision).


Fold the left section, then the right section.


Flip the paper over and cut along the horizontal edge so that you end up with a wedge.


Draw your design on the wedge, similar to what you have drawn in the darker-colored drawing wedge!


Cut along the lines. Unfold the paper, and you're done!

Cut out your printed design


Cut out the square. Make sure you are positioning the square so that the drawing wedge is pointing up. Flip the paper over.


Fold the paper in half diagonally. Your fold should align with the thicker grid line.


Fold the bottom-left corner up towards the top-right corner. The paper triangle should has two grid lines.


Fold the left section, then the right section according to the two grid lines.


Flip the paper over and cut along the lines. Unfold the paper, and you're done!
