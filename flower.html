Draw your own fold-n-cut design


Get a piece of square paper. Turn it rightside up (i.e. you should be facing the "pretty" side!)


Fold the paper up in half.


Mark the 1/3 point from the top of the left edge. Fold the bottom-right corner up towards the 1/3 point.


Fold the bottom left corner over the right edge.


Fold down the top right corner over the left edge.


Cut along the horizontal edge so that you end up with a wedge.


Draw your design on the wedge, similar to what you have drawn in the darker-colored drawing wedge!


Cut along the lines. Unfold the paper, and you're done!

Cut out your printed design


Cut out the square. Make sure you are positioning the square as illustrated above. Flip the paper over.


Fold the paper up in half. Your fold should align with the thicker grid line.


Fold the bottom-right corner up towards the left edge. The right edge should now align with the leftmost grid line.


Fold the bottom left corner over the right edge.


Fold down the top right corner over the left edge.


Cut along the lines. Unfold the paper, and you're done!
