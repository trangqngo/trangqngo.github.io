Paper Flower Designer 5-petal 6-petal

Welcome!

This simple application will help you design your own fold-and-cut paper flowers and snowflakes.
Choose between designing a 5-petal flower and 6-petal flower, read through the instructions and let's get started!
Tip: Make sure to use a fairly thin piece of paper, since it will be hard to fold and cut thicker paper!
